About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense roguelite survival game where you must fight off endless waves of monsters while collecting power-ups and leveling up your character. How long can you survive?

Inspired by popular titles like Vampire Survivors, this game offers addictive gameplay with simple controls but deep strategic elements.

Choose from different characters, unlock powerful weapons and abilities, and face increasingly difficult challenges as you progress through the game.

How to Play

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map.

Your character attacks automatically - position yourself strategically to maximize damage!

Progression

Defeat monsters to gain experience and level up.

Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones with each level.

Key Features

  • Endless waves of diverse monsters to battle
  • Multiple characters with unique abilities
  • Dozens of weapons and power-ups to discover
  • RPG-style progression and leveling system
  • No download required - play instantly in your browser
  • Challenging gameplay with increasing difficulty
